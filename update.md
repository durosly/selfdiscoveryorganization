Don’t just say “Donate”

Say something like:

That emotional connection = more donations.

Donation Page Layout

Hero Section

Headline:

Give Today. Change a Life.

Subheading:
Your support helps us reach vulnerable families, support children’s education, empower young people, and restore hope through the work of Self Discovery Organization.

Primary Button:
Donate Now

Secondary Button:
See Our Impact

Optional Background Image:
Use a strong photo showing real humanitarian work: children, outreach, school support, or family intervention.

⸻

Section 2: Short Mission Statement

Why Your Giving Matters

Self Discovery Organization is a UK-registered charity committed to healing the world of its challenges by uniting people with a sense of purpose. Through education, humanitarian outreach, leadership development, and community support, we are helping individuals and families move from struggle to hope.

⸻

Section 3: Donation Amount Box

Choose an Amount

Use clickable boxes like this:
• £10 — Helps provide essential support materials
• £25 — Supports outreach to vulnerable individuals
• £50 — Helps fund educational or welfare assistance
• £100 — Contributes meaningfully to a family or community intervention
• Custom Amount — £\_\_\_\_

Then add this button below:

Donate Securely

You can also include a checkbox:

☐ Make this a monthly donation

That one is very important because recurring donors help charities plan better.

⸻

Section 4: What Your Donation Supports

Your Gift Makes Real Impact Possible

Create 4 support cards:

1. Send a Child to School Scheme
   Helping disadvantaged children with tuition, learning materials, uniforms, and educational support.

2. Families and Prisoners Initiative
   Providing welfare support for indigent families and dignity items, encouragement, and care for prisoners.

3. Menstrual Hygiene & Youth Empowerment Projects
   Supporting girls and young people with dignity materials, education, and practical empowerment.

4. Community Support & Leadership Development
   Building purpose-driven communities through mentorship, conferences, outreach, and support programmes.

⸻

Section 5: Real Impact Section

Your Generosity at Work

You can add short impact statements like:
• Supported children with school fees and educational needs
• Reached families in need with food and welfare items
• Extended hope and practical care to prisoners
• Empowered young people through purpose and leadership programmes
• Launched humanitarian and community support initiatives across the UK, Nigeria, and Zimbabwe

You can present these as bold figures later when you want:
• 37+ children supported in one intervention
• 25 families reached with food support
• Hundreds fed through outreach programmes

⸻

Section 6: Trust Section

Give with Confidence

Your donation helps us continue impactful work in communities that need it most. Self Discovery Organization is a registered charity, and we are committed to integrity, compassion, and responsible stewardship.

You can add:
• UK Registered Charity
• Secure Payment Processing
• Transparent Use of Funds

If you want, you can also include your charity number here.

⸻

Section 7: Payment Options

Ways to Give

Add payment icons or buttons for:
• PayPal
• Debit/Credit Card
• Bank Transfer

If you are starting with PayPal, this section can simply say:

Donate securely via PayPal

Later, you can expand it when you add Stripe or direct card options.

⸻

Section 8: Call to Action

Be the Reason Someone Finds Hope Today

Every donation, whether big or small, helps us bring relief, restore dignity, and inspire purpose. Partner with us today and become part of a vision that is transforming lives.

Button:
Donate Now

⸻

Suggested Page Order

Use this order on the website: 1. Hero section 2. Mission statement 3. Donation amount section 4. What your donation supports 5. Impact section 6. Trust / credibility section 7. Payment options 8. Final call to action
